What's Linux???

Linux is a free and open-source kernel for UNIX.

You are probably referring to GNU/Linux.

GNU is also a free and open-source project.

GNU is a toolkit, and one of its "tools" is GNU OS.

Most Linux distros are based on it.

Linux distros are operating systems, that use the Linux kernel.
